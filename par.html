Paris Highlights Tour Overview

Immerse yourself in the timeless beauty and charm of Paris on our Paris Highlights Tour. From the iconic Eiffel Tower to the historic Notre-Dame Cathedral, this tour offers an unforgettable exploration of Paris’s most famous landmarks and hidden gems. Whether you’re a first-time visitor or returning to the City of Light, this journey will immerse you in the art, history, and culture of the French capital.

Tour Highlights

  • Eiffel Tower: Soak in stunning panoramic views of Paris from one of the world's most iconic landmarks.
  • Louvre Museum: Explore the world’s largest and most visited art museum, home to masterpieces like the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo.
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral: Step inside the iconic Gothic cathedral, a symbol of French history and culture.
  • Montmartre & Sacré-Cœur Basilica: Wander through the charming streets of Montmartre and visit the beautiful Sacré-Cœur Basilica with sweeping views of Paris.
